About me

Born in Springfield, OH...

I had a pretty normal upbringing in Springfield, OH. I graduated from Shawnee High School in 2014. Afterwards I attended the University of Cincinnati. I soon transferred to Ohio State were I found that maybe pursuing a mathematics degree in college wasn't necessarily my speed. I then dropped out and started to tackle my student loans. That has lead me to this point in my life. I now have been working in the industry of factory work. I realized my potential doesn't stop there so I wanted to challenge myself to be more. From then on, I have been relentlessly trying towards different goals of mine. Last year I started working out again, one of my few goals to get started on. I am currently down 20 pounds, and I would love to drop another 20. Goals I want to achieve over the next few years are learning new skills such as full-stack development, home-inspecting, and maintaining/growing an LLC.
